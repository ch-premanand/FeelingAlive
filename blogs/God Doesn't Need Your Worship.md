# You're Already Worshiping Something

REALIZING GOD DOESN’T NEED YOUR WORSHIP…

He’s already perfect.
Already complete.
Already lacking nothing.

So why does Scripture keep calling you to worship?

Not for Him.
For you.

Worship isn’t God needing attention.
It’s you being realigned.

Because whatever you don’t worship, you replace with something else.

Money.
Status.
Women.
Yourself.

You’re always bowing to something.

The question is:

**What is shaping you?**

Jesus makes this clear:

> "Where your treasure is, there your heart will be also."
> — Matthew 6:21

Worship is what you give your focus, value, and devotion to.

So when God calls you to worship, He’s not feeding His ego.

He’s pulling you out of false gods that will destroy you.

Even the prophets said it:

People who worship idols become like them.

Empty.
Blind.
Lifeless.

Worship isn’t neutral.

It transforms you into what you behold.

So why does God command it?

Because:

* You become what you focus on
* You serve what you love
* You reflect what you worship

And God is the only One who will not corrupt you.

## Blunt Truth

God doesn’t need your worship.

You need to worship something—and He alone is worthy of it.

Worship isn’t about singing songs.

It’s about the alignment of your life.

Your thoughts.
Your actions.
Your desires.

If you’re not consciously worshiping God, you’re unconsciously worshiping something lesser.

And that is what is shaping your life right now.

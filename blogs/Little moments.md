# Little Moments

Rushing through the streets, day after day, week after week, month after month.

What's happening inside me? Something struck inside me that keeps aching more and more.

It's pain, anguish, loneliness, abandonment...

These things are making me crazy from time to time.

First of all, why am I living? What am I living for? What's my role in this universe? What do I actually need?

Is it money? No, it's not. It never made me whole and heartfelt.

Then what is it?

Sometime today, I was going through the streets again, but not in a rush - in exploration, finding something buried deep inside me.

I didn't get to know it, but as I continued, I saw a man sitting at a bus stop. A little smile on his face came from deep inside, with no hurry.

But I saw nothing around him worth mentioning.

I sat a few feet away and tried to start a little chat.

> "Heya, hello."

> "Hello, boy."

> "What is it?"

> "Wh.."

> "I mean, the smile .you look excited."

> "Oh! See over those puppies."

> "Yup, they are very cute and beautiful."

> "It's not about appearance, but see how they are playing and moving together."

He left unnoticed as I looked away.

I don't know why he said that, but how good he is at enjoying little moments.

I think that's life — enjoying little moments, watching our kids grow in front of our eyes, Exploring, getting excited over small things, achieving little things...it continues
